Why AWS?

For 25 years, Amazon has been designing and manufacturing smart products and distributing them—plus billions of other manufacturers’ products—through Amazon’s globally connected distribution network. Amazon Web Services (AWS) was literally born from our own complex automation and factory operations. We use cutting-edge automation, machine learning (ML), artificial intelligence (AI), and robotics.

AWS helps you transform manufacturing operations with the most comprehensive and advanced set of cloud solutions available. Instead of focusing on building and maintaining your infrastructure, you can concentrate your resources on creating new product opportunities, improving operational efficiencies across the value chain, and optimizing production.

Improve product design and production processes

AWS delivers virtually unlimited High Performance Computing (HPC), which lets you innovate quickly without adding infrastructure. When they run HPC in the cloud, your designers, scientists, and engineers can use model-based design and large-scale parallel simulations to solve complex computing challenges.

You can reduce time to results and time to market for products by running more parallel tasks than an on-premises environment would allow. In addition, having optimized central processing unit (CPU), graphics processing unit (GPU), and field programmable gate array(FPGA) servers available on demand helps reduce costs.

  • High Performance Computing (HPC)

  • Reduce time to results and to market

  • Reduce costs

Using electronic design automation (EDA) to develop and deploy smart products can be complex and costly, but using HPC on AWS with Amazon FreeRTOS (real-time operating system, our open-source operating system for multicontrollers) gives you the flexibility to right-size your compute environment to meet the unique needs of each application. By maintaining a universal connection to the AWS cloud platform throughout the process, you can deploy all or just part of your EDA workflow for faster project completion times, increased EDA performance, and reduced costs.

Get smart with your data

Manufacturing is dependent on data – not just gathering it, but using it effectively. To continually get more out of data at every point, you can leverage the full range of AWS tools, including IoT services, edge computing, data lakes, ML, AI, and advanced analytics tools.

From collection to processing, from storage to analysis, from visualization to execution on the production floor, AWS helps you use data to improve smart product development and manufacturing operations.

Advanced analytics can help optimize data gathered from disparate sources and different systems. With Amazon apps such as Kinesis and SageMaker, or services such as AWS Managed Blockchain to digitize data at the source, you can get instantly accessible, end-to-end information.

You can use that data to glean valuable insights: track the supply chain more precisely, predict equipment failures ahead of time, use accurate projections to increase production appropriately, produce higher-quality products, and boost profits.
