Shed your technology debt

Retailers have spent decades mired in the same cycle of investing heavily in on-premises technology to offer great customer experiences. But technology has reached an inflection point. Spending continuously to maintain legacy systems limits innovation and reduces the ability to react to trends and customer desires – or to move to a proactive retail strategy.

AWS cloud services solve that problem. Instead of relying on aging and expensive legacy systems, you can easily pivot to connected, agile, serverless microservices. Your customers can connect their experiences across channels, while you glean priceless insights from available and manageable data.

Secure a better view

Data fragmentation, delays, and the inability to glean strategic insights cripple companies trying to compete in an increasingly tech-dependent marketplace. The potential consequences are huge: Retailers suffer four to five times more harm from a bad consumer experience than what they gain from a good one.

AWS helps you complete your view of the customer journey with real-time, scalable, journey-based data. Leverage the power of the 10,000 data lakes already built on our cloud platforms, and move from customer data in 16-plus systems to real-time insights derived from unified data. This includes actionable IoT data (footfall, location, engagement) for richer, more complete views of the customer journey.

Trusted Logistics

Imagine gaining visibility into every aspect of your supply chain – from vehicle and driver locations, to the status of products en route. With Capgemini’s Trusted Logistics solution, retailers can take a proactive, evidence-based approach to operational efficiency, risk assessment, and disruption avoidance.

Key Features
Trusted Logistics relies on IoT sensors and a secure blockchain framework to monitor and protect all your most valuable assets and keep your business running smoothly.
  • Modular architecture. A modular structure makes this solution easy to adopt and integrate.
  • Cloud-based tracking. Track shipments within a robust IoT infrastructure that decreases process inefficiencies.
  • Unified dashboard. Operations managers gain oversight to monitor container health parameters including temperature, humidity, and location.
  • Real-time alerts. Notifications provide critical information that drives proactive responses.
  • Blockchain incorporation. Blockchain technology enables traceability and proof of authenticity.
Benefits
Securing your supply chain with digital transformation results in increased revenue, fewer fraud and theft events, easy reporting, and efficiency enhancements across your organization.
  • Boost earnings. Digitizing the conventional logistics process increases efficiency and revenue.
  • Avoid damage and spoilage of goods. Real-time monitoring of container parameters keeps shipments in good health.
  • Reduce cargo theft. Remote monitoring with intrusion detection helps avoid theft incidents.
  • Reduce fraud and counterfeiting. Traceable, authenticated supply chain events powered by blockchain help minimize fraud.
  • Improve operational efficiency. Cloud-enabled communication results in better collaboration and faster incident response times.
